## 5. FRAMEWORKS USED WITH EXPLANATION MAPPING

| Framework | How It's Applied in This Output |
|-----------|----------------------------------|
| **Gottman** | Repair bids + turning-towards connection: Signal intention to de-escalate; Lead with validation; Use "we're a team" framing. Avoids stonewalling and criticism by reframing withdrawal as regulation, not rejection. |
| **EFT** | Centers emotional needs and attachment: understanding why Drew needs to be heard (attachment security), why Tony withdraws (self-regulation), and how to reconnect at the emotional level before problem-solving. Safe, attuned language activates receptivity. |
| **Attachment** | Tony shows avoidant attachment under stress (withdraw to regulate). Drew shows anxious attachment (need reassurance of being valued). This output bridges them by acknowledging both styles and creating a window of safety. |
